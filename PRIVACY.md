# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

SCAN-X operates primarily on the local machine and local network.

## Data Collection

SCAN-X does not intentionally collect personal information from users.

The application may process:

- Local network device information
- Hostnames
- IP addresses
- Open port information
- Device metadata required for network assessment

This information remains on the user's system unless exported by the user.

## Analytics

Current versions of SCAN-X do not transmit analytics data to external servers by default.

## Reports

Generated reports are stored locally on the user's computer and remain under the user's control.

## Third-Party Components

SCAN-X includes third-party open-source components including Nmap.

Users should review the respective licenses and privacy practices of included components.

## Contact

Questions regarding privacy may be submitted through the GitHub repository issue tracker.
